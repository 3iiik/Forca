Features

🎯

Focus Zones

🚫

Block Distractions

Ambient Sounds

📊

Focus Score

Daily, weekly, and monthly productivity scoring with beautiful charts.

How It Works

Connect Your Calendar

Link Google Calendar or any iCal feed. Forca reads your schedule.

Forca Detects Free Time

When a meeting ends, Forca automatically detects the gap in your calendar.

Focus Zone Starts

A focus session begins automatically. Apps are blocked, DND is enabled, and you work distraction-free.
